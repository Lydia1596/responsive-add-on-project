Why London?

London, a city rich in history and culture

A visit to London is a must for everyone, as this is one city that will make you fall in love with it so much that you'll want to keep coming back. With stunning architecture, gorgeous gardens, magnificent towers, busy streets, many shops, and some iconic sites, London is everything dreamy.

Big Ben, the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace, the London Eye, the list goes on and on. There is nothing quite like the experience of hopping on a red double-decker bus and seeing some of the most recognisable sights in the world from its top deck.
